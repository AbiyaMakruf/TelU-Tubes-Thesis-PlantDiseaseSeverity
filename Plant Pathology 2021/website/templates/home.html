Welcome

This website is an alternative to the Streamlit demo. It provides an interface to upload images and run object-detection models (YOLO via Ultralytics).

Quick steps
  1. Place your .pt/.pth model under website/models/object_detection/.
  2. Go to Upload & Predict, choose image and (optional) model, then Predict.
